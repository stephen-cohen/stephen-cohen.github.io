Rice response to bacterial blight at high temperature

During even moderate heat stress, rice is more susceptible to bacterial blight. This could severely impact crop productivity in light of climate change. I use transcriptomic, laboratory, and greenhouse experiments to study the mechanisms underlying this phenomenon.
